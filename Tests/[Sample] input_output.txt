Input and output format

>>start
>>[filename]
>>input
[input 1]
[input 2]
>>output
[output]
>>end


------ Example -------

>>start
>>testcase_1.cf
>>input
1
2
3
>>output
You entered: 1
Sum of 2 and 3 is: 5
>>end

>>start
>>testcase_1.cf
>>input
job
lipat
>>output
Enter First Name:
Enter Last Name:
Hello job lipat
>>end
